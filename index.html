About Naaz Commercial Institute

Naaz Commercial Institute is a registered non-profit organization providing skill development, training and mentoring to underserved communities. We partner with local employers and government programs to align our courses with real job opportunities.

Mission

To equip individuals with practical skills, confidence and pathways to sustainable employment.

Vision

A resilient community where every person has access to quality vocational training and opportunities to thrive.

Programs & Training

We offer short-term certification courses and long-term training across multiple trades. Each program includes practical workshops, placement support and follow-up mentoring.

  • Skill Development Workshops — Digital literacy, tailoring, electrical basics, hospitality skills.
  • Entrepreneurship — Small business training, micro-enterprise support.
  • Placement Support — CV building, interview prep, employer connections.

Impact & Stories

Since our founding, we've trained hundreds of learners and helped many secure stable livelihoods. We measure impact through placement rates, income improvement and participant feedback.
