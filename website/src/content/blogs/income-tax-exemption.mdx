Income tax exemption on donations encourages individuals and organizations to support social development initiatives run by NGOs.

## What Is Income Tax Exemption?

Income tax exemption allows donors to claim deductions on eligible charitable contributions under Indian tax laws.

## Why It Matters

Tax exemptions promote responsible giving and help NGOs sustain programs in education, health, and community development.

## Role of NGOs

Registered NGOs follow compliance norms to ensure transparency and eligibility for donor benefits.

## How Donors Benefit

Donors should retain receipts and verify NGO registration before claiming exemptions.
