# Help Street Children: Providing Care and Rehabilitation

Street children face extreme vulnerability and require specialized support. Naaz Commercial Institute provides comprehensive care, rehabilitation, and reintegration programs for street children in rural Bihar.

## Understanding Street Children

Street children live and work on the streets, facing multiple risks and deprivations.

### Categories of Street Children

- **Children of the Streets**: Those who have no home and live on streets
- **Children on the Streets**: Those who work on streets but have homes
- **Runaway Children**: Those who have left abusive or neglectful homes
- **Abandoned Children**: Those whose families cannot care for them

### Causes of Street Life

- **Poverty**: Families unable to provide basic needs
- **Family Breakdown**: Divorce, death, or abuse leading to abandonment
- **Migration**: Rural families moving to cities, leaving children behind
- **Disability**: Children with disabilities abandoned by families
- **Conflict**: Displacement due to violence or natural disasters

### Daily Realities

- **Survival Strategies**: Begging, scavenging, and petty work
- **Health Risks**: Malnutrition, diseases, and injuries
- **Exploitation**: Abuse, trafficking, and forced labor
- **Legal Issues**: Lack of identity documents and protection
- **Educational Deprivation**: No access to schooling or development

## Challenges in Rural Bihar

Rural areas have unique street child issues.

### Urban-Rural Dynamics

- **Seasonal Migration**: Families moving for work, children left behind
- **Market Towns**: Street children in commercial centers
- **Transportation Hubs**: Children at bus stands and railway stations
- **Construction Sites**: Migrant workers' children in temporary settlements

### Local Factors

- **Poverty**: Economic desperation forcing children to streets
- **Lack of Services**: Insufficient shelters and support systems
- **Cultural Stigma**: Shame preventing families from seeking help
- **Limited Awareness**: Communities unaware of available support

### Protection Issues

- **Exploitation**: Children working in hazardous conditions
- **Abuse**: Physical, sexual, and emotional harm
- **Legal Vulnerability**: Lack of guardianship and rights protection
- **Health Crises**: Untreated illnesses and malnutrition

## Naaz Commercial Institute's Street Child Programs

Our NGO provides comprehensive support for street children.

### Rescue and Emergency Care

- **Street Outreach**: Identifying and approaching street children
- **Emergency Rescue**: Removing children from dangerous situations
- **Medical Care**: Immediate health treatment and stabilization
- **Temporary Shelter**: Safe housing during initial rehabilitation

### Rehabilitation Services

- **Shelter Homes**: Long-term residential care facilities
- **Education Programs**: Formal schooling and skill development
- **Psychological Support**: Counseling for trauma and adjustment
- **Family Tracing**: Locating and assessing family situations

### Reintegration Support

- **Family Counseling**: Preparing families for reunification
- **Economic Assistance**: Supporting families to care for children
- **Community Integration**: Building local support networks
- **Follow-up Care**: Monitoring progress after reintegration

### Prevention Initiatives

- **Community Education**: Raising awareness of street child issues
- **Family Support**: Preventing children from becoming street dwellers
- **School Retention**: Keeping children in education
- **Economic Programs**: Reducing poverty that leads to street life
