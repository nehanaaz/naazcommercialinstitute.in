## How to Donate to NGOs in India

Donating is straightforward but requires caution:

### Steps to Donate

1. **Research NGOs**: Check credibility via NGO Darpan or GuideStar India.
2. **Choose Causes**: Align with your values, e.g., education for Naaz.
3. **Methods**:
   - **Online**: Platforms like GiveIndia, Milaap, or NGO websites.
   - **Offline**: Cheques, bank transfers, or in-kind donations.
   - **CSR Funds**: Encourage corporate giving.
4. **Verify Impact**: Ask for reports and receipts.

### Safe Donating Tips

- Avoid unsolicited calls; verify NGO registration.
- Prefer FCRA-compliant NGOs for international aid.
- Use secure payment gateways.

For Naaz Commercial Institute, donations support school supplies and teacher salaries.

## Tax Benefits of Donating to NGOs

India offers incentives to encourage giving:

### Section 80G

- Deductions up to 50-100% on donations to approved NGOs.
- Naaz Commercial Institute is 80G certified, allowing 50% deduction.

### Other Benefits

- **Section 80GG**: For non-homeowners.
- **Corporate Tax**: 100% deduction for CSR spends.
- **Foreign Donors**: Tax treaties apply.

Consult a CA for personalized advice. In 2023, tax benefits spurred ₹50,000 crore in donations.
