# How to Donate to Naaz Commercial Institute

## Steps

1. Choose method.

2. Contact us.

3. Receive certificate.

## Options

Online, offline, in-kind.

## Impact

Education for youth.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Donating

Donating to Naaz Commercial Institute is straightforward and rewarding. Follow these steps to contribute to education and empowerment in rural Bihar.

### Step 1: Choose Your Donation Method

Select the most convenient option for you.

- **Online Donations**: Ideal for quick, secure giving from anywhere.

- **Offline Donations**: Suitable for those preferring traditional methods.

- **In-Kind Donations**: Contribute goods or services.

- **Corporate Donations**: For businesses through CSR.

### Step 2: Decide on Amount and Cause

- **Minimum Donation**: Start with ₹100; no upper limit.

- **Cause Selection**: Specify if you want to support education, health, women empowerment, or general funds.

- **Recurring Donations**: Set up monthly contributions for ongoing impact.

### Step 3: Make the Donation

Execute the donation based on your chosen method.

- **Online**: Go to our donation page [Naaz Commercial Institute Donate](https://naazcommercialinstitute.in/donate), fill the form, and pay via card, UPI, or wallet.

- **Offline**: Write a cheque in favor of "Naaz Commercial Institute" and mail it to our office in Patna, Bihar.

- **Bank Transfer**: Use our account details for direct transfer.

- **In-Kind**: Contact us to arrange delivery of items.

### Step 4: Receive Confirmation and Receipt

- **Instant Confirmation**: Email or SMS acknowledgment.

- **Tax Receipt**: 80G certificate for tax deductions, sent within 15 days.

- **Thank You Note**: Personalized message from our team.

### Step 5: Track Your Impact

- **Updates**: Regular newsletters on program progress.

- **Reports**: Annual impact reports.

- **Stories**: Success stories from beneficiaries.

## Legal Aspects of Donating

Understand the regulations.

### Income-tax Act, 1961

- **Section 80G**: Deduct 50% of donation from taxable income, up to 10% of adjusted gross total income.

- **Eligibility**: Donors must have taxable income; NGOs must be approved.

### Foreign Contributions

- **FCRA Compliance**: For international donors, ensure NGO registration.

### Companies Act, 2013

- **CSR Rules**: Companies can claim donations as CSR expenditure.

### Transparency and Accountability

- **Audited Reports**: Annual financials available.

- **Fund Utilization**: 80%+ on programs, as per norms.
